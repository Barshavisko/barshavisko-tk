Modeling

Part 1 | The Beginning

Modeling in the Source Engine is a pretty simple task, once you have gained the skill of doing such things... Then you can easily make your models and export them into Source, this tutorial is for Garry's Mod. The first step of creating the models is planning the models. Lets start off and say there are some great applications for making models for games, one is blender, an open-free to use modeling software application. The controls and tools take some time to get used to but after a while can be easily mastered. So start off with a plan with your model, plan out your design and write about how its going to work, make a makeshift picture in a painting application or draw it on paper. If you are too lazy to plan like this, then search the internet for referal images for your model.
